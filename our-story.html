How INVESTAGROW
was born

At Investagrow, we're reshaping the real estate and property management scene in Kenya. Founded by our CEO, Francis Maina, our company was born out of a desire to challenge and modernize the property industry. We're all about forward-thinking and bringing fresh perspectives to traditional practices. Clear communication is our hallmark.

We believe in cutting through complexity and providing straightforward, honest guidance to our clients. Whether it's streamlining processes or embracing new technologies, we're dedicated to pushing boundaries and creating a more transparent and efficient industry for property investors and homeowners across Kenya.
